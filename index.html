    About Us

    Life Dream Adventure Pvt. Ltd. is emerging trekking company based in Kathmandu Nepal. The trekking agency is founded by experienced team of guide and porters to offer the professional and high quality service to our clients.

    Our guides are professional and experienced in organizing tours and trekking in Nepal. Similarly, peak climbing and mountaineering adventure guides are experienced. Our guides are trained and qualified by various professional training and skills and received “Trekking Guide License” and Mountain Guide License respectively. All our trekking and climbing guides speak fluent English and good in communication. They have enough knowledge about their field. Along with our expert team we are also an active member of Trekking Agents Association of Nepal (TAAN), Nepal Mountaineering Association (NMA). Himalayan Rescue Association (HRA) and recognized by Nepal Tourism Board (NTB).
